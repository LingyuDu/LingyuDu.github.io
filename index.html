About me

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Embedded Systems Group of the Faculty of Electrical Engineering, Mathematics and Computer Science (EEMCS) at Delft University of Technology, the Netherlands, supervised by Dr. Guohao Lan and Prof. Dr. Koen Langendoen.
My research primarily focuses on building pervasive gaze estimation system, such as improving the resource-efficiency and addressing the potential privacy and security concerns of deep-learning-based gaze estimation models. I am also interested in developing gaze-based human computer interactive systems.
